## 搭配 (Collocations)

### 形容词 + 名词

```
heavy rain (NOT "big rain")
strong coffee (NOT "powerful coffee")
high temperature (NOT "tall temperature")
great importance (NOT "big importance")
fast food (NOT "quick food")
```

### 动词 + 名词

```
make a decision (NOT "do a decision")
take a photo (NOT "make a photo")
do homework (NOT "make homework")
have a conversation (NOT "take a conversation")
give a presentation (NOT "do a presentation")
pay attention (NOT "give attention")
tell a story (NOT "say a story")
keep a promise (NOT "hold a promise")
```

### 副词 + 形容词

```
highly recommended (NOT "strongly recommended")
deeply concerned
bitterly disappointed
fully aware
perfectly normal
strongly believe
widely accepted
```

| 可分离 | 不可分离 |
|--------|---------|

## 学习方法

### 间隔重复 (Spaced Repetition - Anki)

推荐设置:
- 新卡: 每天 10-20 张
- 复习: 每天 50-100 张
- 步骤: 1m 10m 1d

### 词图法 (Word Maps)

```
              ┌─────────────┐
              │  定义/中文    │
              ├─────────────┤
  同义词 ──── →   单词    ← ──── 反义词
              ├─────────────┤
              │  例句        │
              ├─────────────┤
              │  搭配        │
              └─────────────┘
```

### 上下文学习法

- 阅读中遇到生词，先猜词义再查字典
- 记录生词所在原句
- 自己造 3 个不同语境的新句子
