# 写作基础

## 中考英语写作概述
中考英语写作（书面表达）通常占15-20分，要求80-120词。常见题型：**书信、邮件、通知、日记、发言稿、看图作文、材料作文**。

---

## 第一部分：写作评分标准
| 档次 | 分值 | 标准 |
|------|------|------|
| 第一档 | 13-15 | 覆盖所有要点、语法准确、语言得体、逻辑清晰 |
| 第二档 | 10-12 | 覆盖主要要点、少量语法错误、表达基本清楚 |
| 第三档 | 7-9 | 覆盖部分要点、语法错误较多、影响理解 |
| 第四档 | 4-6 | 要点不全、语法错误多、理解困难 |
| 第五档 | 0-3 | 无关内容、不知所云 |

---

## 第二部分：写作流程
### Step 1: 审题（2分钟）
- 确定文体（书信/通知/记叙文等）
- 确定人称（第一人称/第三人称）
- 确定时态（一般现在/一般过去/一般将来）
- 要点标注（圈出题目中的要求点）
### Step 2: 列提纲（3分钟）
- 开头（1句）：引出话题
- 正文（3-5句）：覆盖要点
- 结尾（1句）：总结或期待
### Step 3: 写作（10分钟）
- 用自己熟悉的表达
- 注意语法正确
- 适当使用连接词
### Step 4: 检查（3分钟）
- 查要点是否齐全
- 查语法（时态、单复数、主谓一致）
- 查拼写
- 查字数

## 第三部分：常用模板
### 一、书信/邮件

**开头句**
- I'm writing to invite you to...
- I'm writing to share with you...
- How is everything going? I'm writing to...
- I'm glad to hear from you.

**正文句**
- First of all, we will...
- Besides/What's more, there will be...
- The activity will be held at/in... on...

**结尾句**
- I'm looking forward to your reply.
- I'd appreciate it if you could...
- Write back soon.

**范文**：
```
Dear Tom,

How is everything going? I'm writing to invite you to our school's English Festival.

The festival will be held in the school hall from 2:00 to 5:00 pm on June 15th.
There will be an English speech contest, a singing competition, and a drama performance.
I think it will be a lot of fun.

I'm looking forward to your reply.

Yours,
Li Hua
```

### 二、通知

**开头**
- Attention, please!
- May I have your attention, please?
- Notice is hereby given that...

**正文**
- There will be a... on...
- All the students are required to...
- Everyone is expected to...

**结尾**
- Please be on time.
- Don't miss the chance.

### 三、日记
**格式**：
```
June 15th, 2024   Saturday   Sunny

Today I...
I felt very happy/excited/proud.
What a meaningful day!
```

### 四、发言稿
**开头**
- Good morning, everyone!
- It's my great honor to speak here.
- Today I'm going to talk about...

**结尾**
- That's all. Thank you for listening.
- I hope everyone can...

**范文**：
```
Good morning, everyone!

It's my great honor to speak here. Today I'm going to talk about protecting the environment.

As we all know, our environment is getting worse and worse.
We should do our part to protect it.
First, we can save water and electricity. Second, we should sort the rubbish.
Third, it's a good idea to ride a bike or take a bus instead of driving a car.

Let's take action to make our world a better place.
That's all. Thank you!
```

### 五、看图作文
**写作步骤**：1. 描述图片内容（What can you see in the picture?）2. 分析图片寓意（What does the picture tell us?）3. 发表自己的看法（In my opinion...）

## 第四部分：常用句型
### 一、高级句型
**定语从句**
- I have a friend who is good at English.
- This is the most interesting book that I have ever read.

**状语从句**
- If we work hard, we will achieve our dreams.
- Although it was raining, they went on playing.

**宾语从句**
- I believe that everyone can make a difference.
- He said that he would come.

**It句型**
- It is important for us to learn English well.
- It is necessary that we should protect the environment.

**There be句型**
- There are many ways to solve this problem.
- There will be a sports meeting next week.

### 二、连接词

| 功能 | 连接词 |
|------|--------|
| 递进 | and, also, besides, what's more, moreover, furthermore |
| 转折 | but, however, on the other hand, yet, while |
| 因果 | because, so, therefore, as a result |
| 顺序 | first(ly), second(ly), finally, at last, then, next |
| 举例 | for example, such as, like |
| 总结 | in a word, in short, all in all, in conclusion |

### 三、常用谚语
- Where there is a will, there is a way. （有志者事竟成）
- Practice makes perfect. （熟能生巧）
- Actions speak louder than words. （行胜于言）
- Every coin has two sides. （凡事有利有弊）
- Rome was not built in a day. （罗马不是一天建成的）
- It's never too late to learn. （活到老学到老）
- Nothing is impossible to a willing heart. （心之所愿，无所不成）

## 第五部分：常见题型训练
### 1. 建议信
**要点**：问题描述 + 建议 + 理由

**常用表达**
- You asked me for advice on...
- Here are some suggestions.
- I suggest that you should...
- It would be a good idea to...
- I hope my advice will be helpful.

### 2. 邀请信
**要点**：活动内容 + 时间地点 + 期待

**常用表达**
- I'd like to invite you to...
- We are organizing a...
- The activity will start at...
- I do hope you can come.

### 3. 介绍信
**要点**：介绍内容 + 特点 + 感受

**常用表达**
- I'd like to tell you something about...
- It is located in...
- It is famous for...
- It has a history of...
- I really enjoy...
