# 初中英语练习题与解析

---

## 一、单项填空

**1** —What do you think of the movie?
—It's wonderful. I like ______ very much.
A. it  B. its  C. them  D. their

**2** There ______ a book and two pens on the desk.
A. is  B. are  C. be  D. am

**3** My sister is ______ than me.
A. tall  B. taller  C. tallest  D. the tallest

**4** —______ do you go to the library?
—Once a week.
A. How long  B. How often  C. How far  D. How soon

**5** If it ______ tomorrow, we will stay at home.
A. rain  B. rains  C. will rain  D. rained

**6** He is ______ student in our class.
A. good  B. better  C. best  D. the best

**7** Would you like ______ something to drink?
A. have  B. to have  C. having  D. has

**8** I don't know ______ he will come or not.
A. if  B. whether  C. that  D. what

**9** The book ______ I bought yesterday is very interesting.
A. who  B. which  C. whom  D. what

**10** She ______ her homework before 9 o'clock last night.
A. finish  B. finishes  C. finished  D. has finished

(1) A. food  B. money  C. time  D. water

## 答案解析

### 1
**A**。like是动词，后接代词宾格，指代the movie为单数，用it。
### 2
**A**。There be句型遵循"就近一致"原则，a book是单数，故用is。
### 3
**B**。由than可知用比较级，taller。
### 4
**B**。答语Once a week（每周一次）表示频率，用How often提问。
### 5
**B**。if引导条件状语从句，主句将来时，从句用一般现在时表将来。主语it为三单，用rains。
### 6
**D**。in our class是最高级范围，the best。
### 7
**B**。would you like to do sth.是固定搭配。

### 10
**C**。last night是一般过去时的标志，finished。
