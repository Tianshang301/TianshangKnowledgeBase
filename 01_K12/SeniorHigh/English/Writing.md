### 2. 常见书信类型



#### (1) 建议?*模板框架**?

- 首段：表明写信目的（针对...问题提出建议?

- 中段：具体建议（First... Besides... What's more...?

- 尾段：希望采纳，祝愿



**常用句型**?

- I'm writing to offer you some suggestions/advice on...

- Here are some practical suggestions for you.

- I hope you will find these suggestions helpful.

- It would be a good idea to...

- Why not...? / What about...?



#### (2) 邢请信

**模板框架**?

- 首段：说明活动内容时间地点邀请对?

- 中段：活动安排和亮点

- 尾段：表达期?

**常用句型**?

- I'm writing to invite you to...

- The activity will be held in/at... on...

- We would be greatly honored if you could come.

- I sincerely hope you can come.

- I would appreciate it if you could confirm your participation at your earliest convenience.



#### (3) 道歉?*模板框架**?

- 首段：表达歉意，说明道歉事由

- 中段：解释原因，提出补救措施

- 尾段：再次道歉，请求谅解



**常用句型**?

- I'm writing to apologize for...

- I feel terribly sorry for...

- Please accept my sincere apologies for...

- To make up for my fault, I would like to...

- I hope you can understand my situation.



#### (4) 感谢?*模板框架**?

- 首段：表达感?

- 中段：具体说明感谢的原因和帮助的意义

- 尾段：再次致谢，表达回报之意



**常用句型**?

- I'm writing to express my sincere gratitude to you for...

- I would like to thank you from the bottom of my heart for...

- Words cannot express how grateful I am to you.

- It was very kind of you to...

- I will never forget your timely help.



#### (5) 申请?*模板框架**?

- 首段：说明申请职?项目，来源渠?

- 中段：自我介绍（学历、经历能力格?

- 尾段：表达决心，请求机会



**常用句型**?

- I'm writing to apply for the position of...

- I have a good command of... / I am skilled in...

- I have gained rich experience in...

- I would be grateful if I could be given the opportunity.

- I am confident that I am qualified for the position.



#### (6) 投诉?*模板框架**?

- 首段：说明投诉对象和问题

- 中段：具体描述问题细节和造成的不?

- 尾段：提出期望的解决方案



**常用句型**?

- I'm writing to complain about...

- To my great disappointment, I found that...

- I was extremely dissatisfied with...

- I would appreciate it if you could...

- I hope you will look into this matter and give me a satisfactory reply.



#### (7) 通知

**模板框架**?

- 标题：Notice

## 二读后续?

### 1. 题型特点

- 提供 350 词以内的短文

- 根据内容和所给段落开头语（两段各丢句）续写

- 总词?50 左右



### 2. 评分要点

- **与原文协?：情节人物风格一?

- **逻辑合理**：续写内容与原文自然衔接

- **语言丰富**：词汇多样，句型多变

- **情节完整**：两段之间过渡自然，结尾合理



### 3. 常用描写语汇



#### (1) 情绪描写



| 情绪 | 短语 |

|------|------|

| **高兴** | be overjoyed / wild with joy / a big smile spread across one's face / tears of joy streamed down one's cheeks |

| **悲伤** | burst into tears / choke with sobs / feel heartbroken / with a heavy heart / sorrow filled one's heart |

| **惊讶** | be astonished / be taken aback / one's eyes widened / jaw dropped / in disbelief |

| **害? | be terrified / tremble with fear / one's heart raced / freeze with terror / blood ran cold |

| **愤? | be furious / boil with rage / clench one's fists / glare at / with burning anger |

| **感动** | be deeply moved / touched to tears / a warm feeling spread through one's heart |



#### (2) 动作描写

- **?*：stare at/glance at/catch sight of/fix one's eyes on/peer at/gaze at

- **?*：wander/stagger/stroll/tiptoe/march/dash/dart

- **?*：whisper/murmur/exclaim/shout/mutter/declare

- **?*：reach for/tremble/clutch/grasp/push/pull

- **?*：one's heart beat wildly / heart sank / heart swelled with pride



#### (3) 环境描写

- **天气**：The sun shone brightly. / Dark clouds gathered. / A gentle breeze kissed my face. / Rain poured down.

- **时间**：As night fell, ... / The first light of dawn... / The moon cast a silver glow...

- **空间**：The room was filled with... / In the distance... / At the corner of the street...



### 4. 读后续写模板结构



**第一?（接给定首句）：

- 情节发展：承接前文矛盾或悬念

- 细节描写：动作心理环?

- 过渡句：自然过渡到第二段



**第二?（接给定首句）：

- 高潮或转?

- 结局：解决问题或升华主题

- 结尾：感?启示/感恩



### 5. 常用衔接?

- 时间：then, soon, shortly after, moments later, immediately, eventually

- 转折：however, yet, but, unexpectedly, to one's surprise

- 因果：therefore, as a result, consequently, thus

- 强调：indeed, in fact, actually, obviously

## 三概要写?

### 1. 写作要求

- 阅读丢?50 词左右的短文

- 写出 60 词左右的概要

- 用第三人称，客观概括



### 2. 评分要点

- **要点全面**（覆盖所有主要观点）

- **语言箢?（不添加细节和评论）

- **逻辑连贯**（要点之间过渡自然）

- **独立成文**（不是简单罗列要点）



### 3. 写作步骤

1. **通读全文**：理解主旨和结构

2. **划出要点**：每段的关键句（主题句）

3. **改写要点**：用自己的话重组（同义替捃69句式变换）

4. **连句成段**：使用衔接词连接

5. **棢查字?：控制在 60 词左?

### 4. 常用改写方法



| 方法 | 示例 |

|------|------|

| **同义替换** | important ?significant / because ?due to |

| **词转? | He succeeded ?His success / They agreed / They reached an agreement |

| **句式变换** | 主动→被? 从句→短 |

| **合并句子** | 将多个简单句合并为复合句 |

| **箢化表? | due to the fact that ?because |



### 5. 概要常用句型

- The passage mainly discusses...

- According to the author, ...

- It is pointed out that...

- The writer emphasizes the importance of...

- The passage highlights the problem of...

- In conclusion, the author suggests that...

## 四高分句型库



### 1. 定语从句

- I will never forget the day when I first met you.

- This is a book which is well worth reading.

- He, who is always honest, never tells a lie.



### 2. 名词性从?

- What matters most is that we should never give up.

- It is generally accepted that education plays a vital role.

- The reason why I succeed is that I persist.



### 3. 倒装?

- Only in this way can we solve the problem.

- Not only does he study hard, but also he helps others.

- So important is education that we should not ignore it.



### 4. 强调?

- It is perseverance that leads to success.

- It was not until I failed that I realized the importance of hard work.



### 5. 虚拟语气

- If I were you, I would seize the opportunity.

- I wish I could go back and try again.



### 6. 分词短语

- Faced with difficulties, we should remain optimistic.

- Having finished the work, we went home.

- Given more time, I could have done better.



### 7. with 复合结构

- With the exam approaching, I feel increasingly nervous.

- With all the problems solved, we felt relieved.



### 8. 插入?

- Education, in my opinion, is the key to success.

- This, however, is not the whole story.



### 9. 排比/平行结构

- We should learn to share, to care, and to love.

- The more you read, the more you learn.



### 10. 谚语/格言

- As the saying goes, "Where there is a will, there is a way."

- Practice makes perfect. / Nothing is impossible to a willing heart.
